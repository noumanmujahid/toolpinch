About ToolPinch

ToolPinch is an independent collection of browser-based tools for writing, SEO, keyword planning, PDF files, images, website tasks, developers, calculators, football and everyday utilities.

Our goal is to help visitors complete a clear task without an account or an unnecessarily complicated interface. Every tool page explains what the calculation or transformation does, what information the visitor must provide and where important limitations apply.

Who creates ToolPinch

ToolPinch tools and supporting pages are created and maintained by the ToolPinch editorial team. Corrections, bug reports and source concerns can be sent to toolpinchofficial@gmail.com.

How we build tool pages

We test interactive controls in modern browsers, use transparent formulas and avoid fake search-volume, ranking, plagiarism-detection, live-score or prediction claims. Sports tools calculate user-entered information and clearly distinguish themselves from official organizers and data providers.
