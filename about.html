About ToolPinch

ToolPinch is a free online tools website focused on practical browser-based utilities for SEO, writing, webmaster work, files, calculators and security.

The v2 direction is intentionally more focused than a generic tool website. SEO, writing and webmaster tools come first because those visitors are more likely to return, share pages and generate monetizable traffic.

Publishing approach

Every tool should have a useful page, clear examples, privacy notes, FAQs and internal links to related tools. That gives visitors a better result and gives search engines more context.
