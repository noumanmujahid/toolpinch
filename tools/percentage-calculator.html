Percentage formula

Result = (percentage / 100) x number

Example: 20% of 500 is (20 / 100) x 500 = 100.

FAQs

Can percentages be more than 100?

Yes. A percentage above 100 means the result is larger than the original number.

Can I use decimals?

Yes. You can enter values like 12.5% or 99.99.
