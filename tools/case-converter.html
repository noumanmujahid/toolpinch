Supported text case formats

Uppercase

Turns every letter into capital letters for short labels or headings.

Lowercase

Turns every letter into small letters for cleanup and normalization.

Title case

Capitalizes the first letter of each word for titles and names.

Sentence case

Capitalizes the start of sentences for readable paragraphs.

When to use it

A case converter is helpful when copying text between documents, spreadsheets, websites and social platforms. It saves time when formatting headings, product names, captions or notes.

FAQs

Will title case follow every style guide?

No. This is a simple title case converter. Formal style guides may keep some short words lowercase depending on context.

Can I convert long text?

Yes, but very large text may feel slower on older phones because conversion happens in the browser.
