Build your World Cup 2026 knockout prediction

The 48-team tournament introduces a Round of 32 before the Round of 16, quarterfinals, semifinals and final. Enter the teams in the 16 first-round matchups, choose each winner and continue the prediction until a champion is selected.

The bracket is intentionally editable. Official matchups can depend on group positions and combinations of qualifying third-place teams, so you can update names after the knockout field is confirmed or create a fantasy bracket before then.

Ways to use the bracket

  • Predict the World Cup winner with friends.
  • Create a Messi, Ronaldo, Mbappe or Haaland knockout scenario.
  • Run an office or family prediction challenge without betting.
  • Reuse the bracket for another 32-team football tournament.
