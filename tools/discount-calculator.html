Discount formula

Savings = original price x discount / 100. Final price = original price - savings.

Example: If an item costs 5,000 and the discount is 20%, savings are 1,000 and the final price is 4,000.

Shopping tip

Always compare the final price, not only the discount percentage. A larger percentage on a higher original price can still be more expensive than a smaller discount on a better base price.

FAQs

Does this calculate tax?

No. This calculator shows pre-tax savings and final price. Add sales tax or delivery fees separately.

Can I enter currency symbols?

Enter numbers only. The result works for any currency because the math is the same.
