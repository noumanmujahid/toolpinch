About this tool

A robots.txt file tells crawlers which areas of a site they may crawl. It is useful for basic crawl guidance, but it should not be used to protect private data.

Best for

static websites, small business sites, technical SEO checks and developer cleanup tasks.

FAQs

Does robots.txt hide private pages?

No. Do not use robots.txt as a security system.

Where should robots.txt be uploaded?

Place it at the root of your domain, for example example.com/robots.txt.
