Why repeated sentences matter

Repeated sentences can make a page look rushed and reduce reader trust. They also make content longer without adding value. This checker scans a pasted draft and lists sentences that appear more than once.

Use it before publishing blog posts, landing pages, product descriptions, student drafts or help articles. Removing unnecessary repetition makes the page easier to read and improves the overall quality signal for visitors.

How to use the results

  • Remove accidental repeated sentences.
  • Merge similar sections that say the same thing.
  • Rewrite repeated ideas with clearer examples.
  • Keep important repeated warnings only when they truly help the reader.

Content quality tip

After removing repeated sentences, check readability and word count. A shorter, clearer page often performs better than a long page filled with repeated wording.
