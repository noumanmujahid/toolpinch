Common uses for a word counter

Word counts matter for school assignments, blog posts, product descriptions, meta descriptions, social captions and freelance writing briefs. A quick counter helps you stay inside required limits before submitting or publishing.

Assignments

Check whether an essay is close to the required word count before final editing.

SEO snippets

Use character counts while drafting titles, descriptions and short page summaries.

How the count works

The tool trims extra empty space, then counts groups of text separated by spaces or line breaks. Character count includes spaces because many forms and platforms count spaces as characters.

FAQs

Does this save my text?

No account or database is used in this static version. The calculation happens in your browser.

Why are characters with spaces and without spaces different?

Some platforms count every typed character, including spaces. Others only care about letters, numbers and punctuation.
