Convert World Cup 2026 match times accurately

The 2026 football tournament is played across Canada, Mexico and the United States. Host venues span several timezones, so a kickoff published in New York cannot be converted in the same way as a kickoff in Los Angeles, Mexico City or Vancouver.

Select the timezone used by the match venue, enter the official kickoff date and time, then choose your destination timezone. The result shows the converted local date, time and weekday. Named timezones are used so daylight-saving rules are applied for the selected date.

  • Argentina or Portugal match time in India.
  • United States kickoff time in the United Kingdom.
  • Messi, Ronaldo, Mbappe or Haaland match time locally.
  • New York, Los Angeles, Dallas or Mexico City kickoff conversion.

Accuracy and source note

This tool converts a time that you enter; it does not publish or verify the official fixture schedule. Always copy the date, venue and kickoff from the official competition schedule before converting. Television coverage can begin before kickoff, so check whether a listing refers to the match or pre-match programming.
