Create a football starting eleven

Select a formation, enter 11 player names and generate a structured starting lineup. The builder supports popular shapes including 4-3-3, 4-4-2, 4-2-3-1, 3-5-2 and 3-4-3.

Use the tool to plan a local team, create a World Cup dream XI, compare tactical options or build a lineup featuring players such as Messi, Ronaldo, Mbappe, Haaland and Lamine Yamal. The result is a clean text summary that can be copied and shared.

Formation tips

  • 4-3-3 offers width and three forward positions.
  • 4-4-2 uses two forwards and a balanced midfield line.
  • 4-2-3-1 separates two deeper midfielders from three attacking roles.
  • 3-5-2 adds midfield numbers with two forwards.

What the builder does not do

The tool does not evaluate player quality, eligibility or tactical chemistry. It simply organizes the names you enter into the selected formation. Coaches and organizers should verify official squad and competition rules separately.
