What this text similarity checker does

This tool compares two pasted text blocks and estimates how similar they are based on shared words and exact matching sentences. It is useful when reviewing drafts, checking whether two versions are too close or comparing source notes with a rewritten paragraph.

The checker runs in your browser and only compares the text you paste. It does not search the internet or replace a formal editorial review. Use it as a practical writing helper before publishing.

Good use cases

  • Compare an original draft with a revised version.
  • Check whether rewritten content still follows a source too closely.
  • Find matching sentences between two text blocks.
  • Review guest posts or team drafts for repeated wording.

Responsible writing note

If you use facts, research, quotes or unique ideas from another source, give proper credit. The best content is useful, accurate and written for readers first.

FAQs

Is Text Similarity Checker free?

Yes. The tool is free to use in your browser.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The page is responsive. Some large file tools may work better on desktop browsers.

Does this check the whole web?

No. It only compares the two text blocks you paste into the tool.

What similarity score is acceptable?

There is no universal score. Review matching sentences and rewrite sections that are too close to source material or repeated unnecessarily.
