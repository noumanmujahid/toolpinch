Password safety tips

Use unique passwords

Do not reuse the same password across email, banking, social media and shopping accounts.

Prefer length

Long random passwords are usually stronger than short complicated ones.

For important accounts, store passwords in a trusted password manager and enable two-factor authentication where available.

Do not send generated passwords through chat, email or screenshots unless you fully trust the destination.

FAQs

Is the password sent to a server?

No. This static tool uses browser-side JavaScript and does not upload generated passwords.

What length should I use?

Use at least 12 characters for ordinary accounts. For important accounts, 16 characters or more is better.
