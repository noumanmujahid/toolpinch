Calculate a player’s age on any match date

Enter a football player’s date of birth and a match or tournament date. The calculator returns the exact age in years, months and days, plus the total number of days between the dates.

This is useful for questions such as Messi’s age during a World Cup match, Ronaldo’s age on a scoring-record date, or whether a youth player meets an age cutoff. Always verify the date of birth with a reliable biography or official team source.
