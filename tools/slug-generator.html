What makes a good URL slug

A URL slug is the readable part of a page address. For example, a page about meta descriptions might use best-meta-description-length. Clear slugs help visitors understand the page topic and make internal links easier to read.

A good slug is short, lowercase and focused on the main topic. Avoid unnecessary dates, stop words and special characters unless they are needed for meaning.

Slug writing tips

  • Use words that describe the page topic.
  • Separate words with hyphens.
  • Keep slugs stable after publishing.
  • Avoid changing indexed URLs without redirects.

SEO note

A clean slug is only one small SEO factor, but it supports clarity. Pair it with a useful title tag, meta description and page content.
