When to use JPG to PDF

JPG to PDF is useful when you need to send photos, scanned pages, receipts or image documents in a format that is easier to print or share. A PDF keeps the image in a document-style layout.

Tips for better PDFs

  • Use clear, readable images.
  • Compress large images before creating a PDF.
  • Check page orientation before saving.
  • Avoid sensitive documents on shared computers.
