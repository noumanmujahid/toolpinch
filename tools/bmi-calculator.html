How to use the BMI calculator

  1. Enter your weight in kilograms.
  2. Enter your height in centimeters.
  3. Click calculate to see your BMI and category.
BMI formula: weight (kg) / height (m)²

BMI categories for adults

Below 18.5

Underweight range.

18.5 to 24.9

Healthy weight range.

25 to 29.9

Overweight range.

30 or more

Obesity range.

Important note

BMI is useful for quick screening, but it does not tell the full health story. Athletes, older adults, pregnant people and growing children may need different measurements or professional guidance.

FAQs

Can BMI be used for children?

Children and teenagers usually need age-and-sex adjusted BMI charts. This calculator is designed for quick adult estimates.

Why does height use centimeters?

Centimeters are common internationally and can be converted easily to meters for the BMI formula.
