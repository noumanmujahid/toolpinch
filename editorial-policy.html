Editorial Policy

ToolPinch publishes interactive tools and explanatory guides designed to help visitors complete practical tasks. Pages should be useful without requiring a search-engine visit, and titles must accurately describe what the page provides.

AI writing standards

AI writing tools are presented as draft helpers only. Pages must avoid claims about bypassing plagiarism checks, defeating AI detectors, guaranteed rankings, guaranteed approval or automatic earnings. Visitors are reminded to review facts, originality and sensitive claims before publishing.

Accuracy and testing

Calculators use formulas shown or explained on the page. Browser tools are tested with valid, empty and incorrect inputs. Results that depend on user-entered figures are labelled clearly and should be checked before important use.

Sources and current information

When a page discusses competition formats, search guidance or third-party requirements, we prefer primary sources such as official organizer regulations and product documentation. Time-sensitive facts are dated. ToolPinch does not present copied news, unofficial live scores or manually maintained player totals as current data.

Independence and trademarks

ToolPinch is not affiliated with FIFA, football teams, players or tournament organizers. Names such as FIFA World Cup, Messi and Ronaldo are used descriptively to explain relevant tools and common comparison examples. We do not use official tournament logos, team crests or unlicensed player photographs.

Corrections

Send correction requests to toolpinchofficial@gmail.com with the page URL and supporting detail. We review material errors and update the page when a correction is verified.
