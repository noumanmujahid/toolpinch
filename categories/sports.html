Football tools for World Cup and everyday match analysis

This category combines timely World Cup 2026 utilities with evergreen football tools. Convert match times across host cities, calculate group tables, rank third-place teams, predict a knockout bracket, compare player statistics and build a starting eleven.

The tools are interactive and use information entered by the visitor. ToolPinch does not copy live scores, publish betting tips or pretend to provide official tournament data. This keeps each page useful after the current tournament and makes its limits clear.

Recommended World Cup workflow

  • Confirm a fixture using the official competition schedule.
  • Convert the host-city kickoff with the Match Time Converter.
  • Test group outcomes with the Standings Calculator.
  • Rank third-place scenarios before the final group matches.
  • Build a Round of 32 prediction after matchups are confirmed.

Player and team tools

Use the Player Comparison Tool for Messi vs Ronaldo or Mbappe vs Haaland statistics, the Goals Per Game Calculator for scoring rates, and the Lineup Builder for a custom starting XI. Enter figures from a reliable source and keep the comparison period consistent.

ToolPinch is an independent website and is not affiliated with or endorsed by FIFA, any tournament organizer, team or player. Names are used descriptively. No official logos or player photographs are used.
