Privacy Policy

ToolPinch provides browser-based tools for common writing, file, website and calculation tasks. Many tools process text or files directly in your browser, which helps keep routine work simple.

Information you enter

ToolPinch does not ask visitors to create an account. Avoid entering private, confidential or sensitive information into any online tool unless you are comfortable with the tool and device you are using.

Files and browser processing

Some file tools use your browser to read file details or create previews. Large files may depend on browser support and device performance. Do not use shared devices for private files.

Third-party services

Some features may request limited data from external services through your browser. Dictionary, synonym and antonym tools may request public word data from dictionary or word-relation APIs. QR code generation may request a QR image from a third-party QR service. Vercel Analytics and Speed Insights are used to understand page views and performance.

Advertising

ToolPinch may add advertising in the future. If advertising services are added, this policy should be updated with any relevant partner or cookie information.

For privacy questions or correction requests, contact toolpinchofficial@gmail.com.
