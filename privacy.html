Privacy Policy

ToolPinch provides browser-based tools for writing, files, websites, calculations and sports scenarios. Many tools process text, files or values directly in your browser.

Information you enter

ToolPinch does not require an account. Avoid entering private, confidential or sensitive information into any online tool, especially on a shared device. Sports tools use the names, dates, scores and statistics you enter only to create the result shown in your browser.

Files and browser processing

Some file tools use your browser to read file details or create previews. Large files may depend on browser support and device performance.

Third-party services

Dictionary and word-relation tools may request public data from external APIs. QR generation may request an image from a third-party QR service. Vercel Analytics and Speed Insights help us understand page views and performance. The football tools added in v3.9 do not request live scores or player data from an external sports API.

Advertising

ToolPinch may add advertising in the future. If an advertising service is enabled, this policy will be updated with relevant partner, cookie and consent information before or alongside that change.

Contact

For privacy questions or correction requests, contact toolpinchofficial@gmail.com.
