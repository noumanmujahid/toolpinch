Contact ToolPinch

For corrections, bug reports, privacy questions and tool suggestions, contact toolpinchofficial@gmail.com.

Please include the tool name, page URL, browser or device and enough detail to reproduce the issue. For a calculation concern, include the input values and the result you expected.

Useful reasons to contact us

  • Report a broken control, incorrect calculation or mobile layout problem.
  • Suggest a practical new tool or supporting guide.
  • Request a factual, spelling or source correction.
  • Ask a privacy or data-use question.
  • Report a trademark, copyright or attribution concern.

What not to send

Do not email passwords, private documents, financial details, medical records or other sensitive information. ToolPinch does not provide emergency, legal, financial or official tournament support.

Review process

Messages are reviewed for actionable detail. Verified errors are prioritized, while general ranking, advertising and partnership requests may not receive an immediate response.
